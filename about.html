About This Website

EnglishWithTunahan

EnglishWithTunahan is a free English learning platform created for Turkish middle school students. Its goal is to make English lessons simpler, more organized, and easier to access.

📚 Features

  • Grade 5–8 Units
  • PDF Notes
  • Worksheets
  • Slides & Presentations
  • Quizlet Sets
  • More resources coming soon
